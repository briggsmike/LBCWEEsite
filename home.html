Welcome to WEEschool

Providing care, education, and development for children and their families. Our goal is to have a program that allows every child to grow and develop as Jesus did - "in wisdom and stature, and in favor with God and man" (Luke 2:52). We long to join you in providing the loving care and Christian guidance that your children need in their formative years. One of the most important things we want your child to learn is that they are unique - made by God for a special purpose. To guide us in this endeavor, we use A Beka Book in each of our classrooms, along with Gods word. A Beka Book is a curriculum that provides our school with God-honoring, higher quality textbooks and teaching aids that help us fulfill the goal of our ministry, which is to teach children about who they are in Christ. A Beka Book has been developed and refined for over 50 years. Between this curriculum and our outstanding teachers, your child will thrive academically and leave our program knowing that he or she was made with an incredible purpose.
